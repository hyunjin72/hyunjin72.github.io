Hyunjin Seo

I am a Ph.D student at Machine Learning and Intelligence Lab (MLILAB) at KAIST, under the supervision of Prof. Eunho Yang. My research centers on the practical applications of structured data and geometric deep learning.

My work involves developing data and model pruning techniques to address the computational challenges associated with training large-scale graphs. Additionally, I have focused on enhancing representation learning on text-attributed graphs through the integration of large language models (LLMs). Currently, my primary research interest lies in molecular conformation prediction using graph neural networks (GNNs).
