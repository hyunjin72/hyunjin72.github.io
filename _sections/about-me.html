I am a Ph.D student at Machine Learning and Intelligence Lab (MLILAB) at KAIST, under the guidance of Prof. Eunho Yang. My research interests are centered on the practical deployment of graph-structured data and graph neural networks (GNNs).

My work has involved graph sparsification techniques to mitigate the computational challenges of training large-scale graphs. Moreover, I have focused on calibrating GNNs to ensure their reliable utilization in downstream decision-making process. Recently, my primary focus is on addressing graph-related tasks under the supervision of large language models.
